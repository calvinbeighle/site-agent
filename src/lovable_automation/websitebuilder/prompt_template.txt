## 2 Task Overview

### 2.1 Extract & Elevate Copy  
• Crawl **{{SITE_URL}}**, rewrite for clarity + SEO, keep brand voice, ditch fluff.

### 2.2 Information Architecture  
• Pages: **Home, About, Services, Contact** ± Blog, Case-Studies, FAQ, Resources.

### 2.3 Design & Build
| Page | Minimum |
|------|---------|
| **Home** | Hero headline; **verified Unsplash hero (HTTP 200)**; elevator pitch; benefits grid; strong CTA. |
| **About** | Brand story; real team bios **(no AI faces)**. |

**Sub-page minimum:** ≥ 60 words, 1 live Unsplash image (`alt` with photographer credit if people shown), **1 Industry-Feature widget**, plus a bold KPI call-out (e.g., “10 % cost-to-serve reduction”). No “Coming soon” placeholders.

**Industry-Feature widgets** (pick as relevant)  
• Real Estate → gallery | Law → secure intake form | Consulting → booking calendar | Logistics → inventory gallery | Construction → project slider | etc.

### 2.4 Visual & Interaction Standards  
• **Imagery first:** cache ≥ 8 hi-res Unsplash URLs per main page; reject dead links; no AI faces.  
  – **Real-headshot rule:**  
    • Portraits allowed **only** if `alt` includes “Photo by <Name> on Unsplash”.  
    • Reject portraits whose URL or `alt` contains “generated”, “synthetic”, “ai”, “profile-photo”, or “thispersondoesnotexist”.  
    • If no qualifying portrait is found, use a silhouette or contextual team photo—never an AI headshot.  

• **Typography:** choose a free Google-font pair that matches award winners in **{{INDUSTRY}}** (serif+sans for law, grotesk for tech, mono+grotesk for data). Ship 400 / 600 / 700 weights; pass contrast.  
• **Palette:** use brand colors that pass contrast, else accessible blue set.  
• **Contrast:** automated audit ≥ 4.5 : 1 (normal) / 3 : 1 (large).  
• Buttons & Nav contrast: Every button or text-link (header, footer, forms, body CTAs) must meet WCAG-AA contrast in both default and hover/focus states. Header/nav items must still pass in transparent (un-scrolled) and solid (scrolled) header modes.


• **Logo & nav contrast:** Logo mark and all nav text/icons must pass WCAG-AA in default & hover for both transparent (un-scrolled) and solid (scrolled) header states. Provide an alt-color or monochrome SVG swap if the brand logo is too dark or too light.  
• **Fallback overlay:** If logo + nav still fail on a transparent hero, apply a 60-80 % dark (or light) overlay behind the header until scroll to guarantee contrast.  

• **Layout:** grid, generous whitespace, sticky header, fluid breakpoints.  
• **Interactive elements:** subtle ≤ 300 ms animations, no overlap.  
• **Accessibility:** semantic HTML + ARIA, WCAG 2.1 AA.

### 2.5 Technical  
• Modular code; forbid problematic libs (e.g., **lucide-react**).  
• SEO: semantic headings, meta, JSON-LD schema for Organisation & Services.  
• Perf: WebP, lazy-load, ≤ 100 KB JS; target LCP < 1.8 s on 3 G.  
• Responsive; tested on major browsers & devices.  
• Vercel-ready.
